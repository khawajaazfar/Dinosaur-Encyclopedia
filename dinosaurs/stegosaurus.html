Stegosaurus

Dino Profile

  • Period: Late Jurassic
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Location: North America, Europe

About the Stegosaurus

Stegosaurus is famous for the two rows of large, diamond-shaped plates along its back. The purpose of these plates is debated, with theories ranging from defense to thermoregulation or display. It also had a dangerous tail tipped with four sharp spikes, known as a "thagomizer."

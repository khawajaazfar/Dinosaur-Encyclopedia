Velociraptor

Dino Profile

  • Period: Late Cretaceous
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Location: Mongolia, China

About the Velociraptor

Despite popular depictions, the real Velociraptor was only about the size of a turkey. It was a feathered dinosaur with a long, stiff tail. Its most famous feature was a large, sickle-shaped claw on each foot, which it likely used to pin down and dispatch prey. They were intelligent and probably hunted in packs.

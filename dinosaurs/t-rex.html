Tyrannosaurus Rex

Dino Profile

  • Period: Late Cretaceous
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Location: North America

About the T-Rex

The Tyrannosaurus Rex, often called T-Rex, was one of the largest land carnivores of all time. It had a massive skull balanced by a long, heavy tail. Its powerful jaws contained around 60 serrated teeth, some up to 12 inches long, which it used to crush bone and tear flesh.

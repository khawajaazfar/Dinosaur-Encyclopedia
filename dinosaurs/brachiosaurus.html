Brachiosaurus

Dino Profile

  • Period: Late Jurassic
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Location: North America, Africa

About the Brachiosaurus

Brachiosaurus was a massive sauropod known for its giraffe-like build, with long forelegs and an extremely long neck. This build allowed it to browse for leaves high up in the treetops, feeding on vegetation that was out of reach for most other dinosaurs. It was one of the tallest dinosaurs known.
